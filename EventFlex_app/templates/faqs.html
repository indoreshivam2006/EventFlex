Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventFlex

Getting Started

How do I sign up on EventFlex?
Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. You'll need to choose whether you're an event organizer or event professional, provide your email, create a password, and complete your profile. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.
Is EventFlex free to use?
Yes! EventFlex offers a free plan that includes up to 5 job posts per month for organizers. Event professionals can create profiles and apply for jobs completely free. We also offer premium plans with additional features for growing businesses.
What types of events can I find staff for?
EventFlex supports all types of events including weddings, corporate events, conferences, exhibitions, concerts, private parties, festivals, and more. You can find specialized staff for any event type.

For Event Organizers

How do I post a job?
After logging in, go to your Organizer Dashboard and click "Create New Job". Fill in the event details including title, description, date, location, roles needed, and compensation. You can save as draft or publish immediately to start receiving applications.
How does the payment system work?
EventFlex uses a secure escrow system. When you accept a staff member, you commit the payment amount to escrow. After the event is completed, you release the payment which goes directly to the staff member's wallet. They can then withdraw to their bank account. This protects both parties.
Can I hire multiple staff for one event?
Absolutely! You can accept as many applications as you need for a single event. The platform automatically calculates the total payment commitment and ensures you have sufficient funds before accepting each application.
What if I need to cancel after accepting staff?
If you need to cancel, contact the staff member through our messaging system as soon as possible. Depending on the cancellation timing and terms agreed upon, you may need to provide partial compensation. Our support team can help mediate if needed.

For Event Professionals

How do I find jobs?
Log in to your Staff Portal and browse available jobs. You can filter by event type, date, location, and pay rate. Use the search function to find specific opportunities. Our AI matching also recommends jobs that fit your profile.
How do I get verified?
Go to your profile settings and click "Get Verified". You'll need to upload government-issued ID, proof of address, and professional credentials. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours. Verified professionals get 3x more job offers!
When do I get paid?
After completing the event, the organizer releases the payment from escrow. Funds are immediately added to your EventFlex wallet. You can then withdraw to your bank account anytime. Bank transfers typically complete within 1-2 business days.
Can I work multiple events at once?
Yes! As long as the event dates and times don't conflict, you can apply for and work as many events as you want. Many professionals work 10-15 events per month on EventFlex.

Payments & Security

Is my payment information secure?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) to protect all financial data. We never store complete payment card information. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways compliant with PCI DSS standards.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. For withdrawals, you'll need to provide bank account details (account number, IFSC code).
Are there any fees?
EventFlex charges a 5% service fee on successful bookings. This fee covers platform maintenance, payment processing, customer support, and continuous feature improvements. There are no hidden charges.
What if there's a payment dispute?
If there's a dispute between organizer and staff, contact our support team immediately. We review all communication, event details, and evidence from both parties. As funds are held in escrow, we can mediate fairly and release payments according to our terms of service.

Technical Support

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us through the Contact page, email us at support@eventflex.com, or use the in-app messaging system. Premium users get priority support with response times under 2 hours during business hours.
Is there a mobile app?
Our platform is fully mobile-responsive and works great in mobile browsers. Native iOS and Android apps are currently in development and will be launched soon. You can sign up for early access notifications in your account settings.
