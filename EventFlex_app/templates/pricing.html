Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a commission on payments?
Yes, we charge a small service fee of 5% on successful bookings. This helps us maintain the platform and ensure quality service.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your subscription fee.
How does payment escrow work?
When you hire staff, payment is held securely in escrow. After the event is completed and confirmed, funds are released to the staff members automatically.
